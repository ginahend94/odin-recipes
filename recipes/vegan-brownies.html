Vegan Brownies

Description

Fudgy, chocolatey, delicious vegan brownies. Made with all-vegan ingredients, enjoy the chocolate without the cruelty!

Adapted from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 2 Tbsp vegan butter
  • 2 Tbsp water
  • 1 1/2 c semisweet chocolate chips (fair trade)
  • 1/2 c applesauce
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2/3 c all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease an 8x8 inch square pan.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar, butter and water. Cook over medium heat until boiling. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted and smooth. Mix in the applesauce and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda and salt; stir into the chocolate mixture. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until brownies set up. Do not overbake! Cool in pan and cut into squares.
